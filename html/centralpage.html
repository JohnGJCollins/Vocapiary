Have you ever read a book and not recognized a word, which then caused you to stop reading, destroy your immersion, pick up your phone, and look up the definition? If you're honest with yourself, and you've read a single book, this has happened to you.

This is the problem that Vocapiary solves. If you learn a book's vocabulary before you read it, you will never have to break your immersion due to vocabulary deficiency.
